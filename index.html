Hello!

My name is Tesia (she/her), and I'm an undergraduate college student who, quite frankly, needs to let it all out. I study public policy and spend an unreasonable amount of time thinking about how laws and institutions quietly shape who gets heard and who gets left out.

"Me? Gongaga" - Zack Fair, Final Fantasy VII Crisis Core, 2007

Besides policy and politics, I love visual art. In my free time, I like to paint and get charcoal all over my hands while making portraits. Additionally, I studied graphic design for half a decade. Visual art and graphic design help me process what I am learning, so you will see some of my notebook doodles in my blog posts.
