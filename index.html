The Bird's Eye View

My name is Tesia (she/her), and I'm an undergraduate college student who, quite frankly, needs to let it all out. I study public policy and spend an unreasonable amount of time thinking about how laws and institutions quietly shape who gets heard and who gets left out while the media strategically sways what you hear.

In my blog, I collect a large variety of moments, connect them to larger structures of power, and practice the kind of clear, grounded storytelling I hope to carry into the work of fighting for better policy from the inside.

Besides policy and politics, I love visual art. In my free time, I like to paint and get charcoal all over my hands while making portraits (that's a lie). Additionally, I studied graphic design for half a decade. Visual art and graphic design help me process what I am learning, so you will see some of my notebook doodles in my blog posts.
